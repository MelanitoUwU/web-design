Learn about Montserrat Typeface ᕙ( •̀ ᗜ •́ )ᕗ

Montserrat is a geometric sans-serif typeface designed by Julieta Ulanovsky in 2011. It was inspired by the old posters and signs in the Montserrat neighborhood of Buenos Aires, Argentina. The typeface is widely used for its clean lines and modern aesthetic.

It's commonly used for both digital and print media, including websites, branding, and advertising. Its clear and legible design makes it a favorite for headlines and body text alike.

Julieta Ulanovsky
Is an Argentine graphic and typeface designer based in Buenos Aires. Her work on Montserrat has made it a popular choice among web designers and has contributed to its widespread use on websites across the world.
